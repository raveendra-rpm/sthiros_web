OUR STORY

BFSI & Fintech

Compliance moves at RBI speed. Growth moves at UPI speed. We build for both, on the same balance sheet.

D2C & Retail

The brand's built. The backend running it is still Shopify, Meta Ads, and a 3PL dashboard that don't talk to each other. We make them add up to one number you can act on.

Energy, Mining & Utilities

CUF, AT&C losses, stripping ratio. The model promised a number, and the asset is delivering a different one. We close that gap with people who read a P&L in megawatts.

GCC

Over 1,700 GCCs and 1.9 million people in India, and HQ still asks the same question every quarter: what's the value story? We build the one that survives budget season.

Healthcare & Life Sciences

ALOS, USFDA scrutiny, NABH accreditation. Thin margins and real stakes, in the same sentence. We build operating discipline that respects both.

Manufacturing

The plant's running at capacity. OEE tells a different story. We go after the gap between what the line can do and what it's actually doing.

Telecom & Digital Infrastructure

ARPU sits around ₹198 and 5G capex doesn't care. We help operators and infrastructure investors make the network pay for itself faster.

Travel & Hospitality

Occupancy recovered years ago. GOP hasn't caught up. We go after the RevPAR-to-bottom-line gap that OTA commissions keep eating.

Public Sector & Government

The scheme got Cabinet approval. The DPR, the UC cycle, and the last mile didn't get the memo. We build the delivery machinery in between.

Real Estate / EPC / Construction

RERA fixed the transparency problem. It didn't fix the ₹111-lakh-crore execution problem. We help developers and EPC players build what the model promised, on the timeline it promised.
